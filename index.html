About TEDxBIT Noida

TEDxBITNoida 2025 marks the first-ever TEDx event at the BIT Noida campus. Rooted in TED’s philosophy of “Ideas Worth Spreading,” our event is dedicated to showcasing stories and ideas that challenge perspectives and inspire action.

Our theme this year, Breaking Barriers, celebrates individuals who have overcome limitations, defied conventions, and reimagined what’s possible. Through inspiring talks, performances, and conversations, TEDxBITNoida aims to ignite curiosity, spark dialogue, and foster a community of bold thinkers and doers.

About TED

TED stands for Technology, Entertainment, and Design- three broad fields that together shape our world.

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to spreading ideas. What began over 30 years ago as a small conference in California has evolved into a global movement, with TED Talks, TEDx events, and a wide range of initiatives that share ideas across every discipline and culture.

At its core, TED is about the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives, and ultimately, the world.

About BIT Noida

The Birla Institute of Technology, Noida (BIT Noida) is an off-campus centre of BIT Mesra, Ranchi, and a distinguished institution in the National Capital Region. Upholding BIT Mesra’s legacy of academic excellence, BIT Noida offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in computer applications, business management, and related fields.

With modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and an industry-aligned curriculum, BIT Noida emphasizes innovation, research, entrepreneurship, and leadership. The campus continues to grow as a hub for aspiring professionals to thrive, collaborate, and contribute meaningfully to a rapidly evolving global landscape.

Theme

BREAKING BARRIERS

In a world that's constantly evolving, we find ourselves at the intersection of tradition and innovation. This year's TEDxBIT Noida explores ideas that transcend conventional limits - in technology, society, art, and human potential.

Join us as we examine how breaking barriers can lead to groundbreaking discoveries and transformative experiences.
